Detailed Description

John is a senior social media analyst for the National Basketball Association. He graduated from the University of North-Carolina's Business School with a 3.2 GPA. During his years as an undergraduate student John developed a passion for games, ending up doing a couple of reviews for the campus magazine.

After working with several organization for 5 years, John returned to University of North-Carolina and completed a 3 year Social Psychology Masters Degree.

John returned to the work force and from 2003 to 2011 he has been collaborating with various sports organization to help them design programs that will increase fan engagement.

After the explosion of social media John brought his skills in the NBA. Until now he has help shape various NBA events such as NBA All Star Weekend and NBA Cares.

Although John loves his job, this gives him very little spare time, but he always plays a quick game after he finishes a big project.
